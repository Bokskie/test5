FOOD LIST RECIPES

  • Beef Steak

    350 g Beef Steak (thin slices) ¼ cup Grape Vinegar add Salt 2 s Big Onions 1 teaspoon Cinnamon 1 tablespo… Curry ¼ cup Water 1 teaspoon Mustard Powder ½ teaspoon Black Pepper 1 teaspoon Sugar 4 tablespo… Vegetable Oil

  • Ginataang Baboy

    ¼ kilogram Pork (thinly sliced or diced, small) ½ cup Shrimp Paste (bagoong) 10 s Finger Chilies (sliced julienned) 1 Onion (peeled and thinly sliced) 6 cloves Garlic (crushed) 3 tablespoons Ginger (peeled and minced) 3 cups Coconut Milk add Salt and Pepper (to add taste) 2 tablespoons Canola Oil

  • Adobong Baboy

    500 g Pork Liempo (cut into 2-inch chunks) 2 tablespoons Oil 4 cloves Garlic 200 g Chicken Liver 2 pcs Labuyo 2 pcs Laurel Leaf 1 tablespoon Whole Peppercorn ⅓ cup Soy Sauce ½ cup Vinegar 1 cup Water

  • Tinolang Isda

    2 tablespoons Canola Oil 1 Medium Red Onion (peeled, quartered) 1 1-inch piece Ginger (peeled, cut into strips) 4 cups Fish Stock (or water) 1 Small Green Papaya 2 tablespoons Patis (fish sauce, or to taste) 4 s Small Tanigue Steaks ½ cup Malunggay Leaves

  • Hotsilog

    Hotsilog is a meal composed of hotdogs, garlic fried rice, and fried egg. In a Filipino household, this is commonly eaten for breakfast with a condiment of banana ketchup and some pickled shredded papaya (atchara) on the side.

  • Lumpia

    3 cups Flour 3 cups Water 1 tablespoon Salt 2 pounds Ground Pork 1 Onion (whole chopped) 1 stick Carrots (grated) 1 tablespoon Garlic (minced) 1 Egg 1 teaspoon Salt add Rice Wine Vinegar 1 teaspoon Olive Oil to taste Salt to taste Garlic (minced) to taste Black Pepper 2 s Hot Chiles

  • Tortang talong

    1 Eggplant 1 Egg ¼ teaspoon Salt ⅛ teaspoon Black Pepper (ground) ¼ cup Oil (cooking)

  • Chicken Curry

    1 kilo chicken 2 medium sized potatoes chopped 1 big carrot sliced 1 tbsp garlic minced 3 stalks celery cut into 2 inches length 1 medium onion chopped 1 small red bell pepper cut into cubes 2 tbsp fish sauce 1 cup coconut milk 2 tbsp curry powder 1 thumb ginger cut into strips 1 cup water

  • Chicken liver

    1 pound chicken livers - rinsed, trimmed, and patted dry 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil 2 teaspoons lemon juice ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste 6 cloves garlic, minced

  • Ube Binignit

    ½ lb (about 3 pieces medium-size) purple yam (ube) or any sweet potatoes of choice ½ lb taro rootBinignit Ginataan Recipe 02 3 pieces large ripe saba bananas 2 cups (or 1 can) coconut milk 1 cup packed brown sugar 1 ½ cup (or 1 jar) sago in syrup or tapioca pearls in syrup ½ cup glutinous or sweet rice 8 cups water

  • Banana Que

    10 pcs Ripe Saba Banana (or semi-ripe) ¼ cup Brown Sugar 2 cups Vegetable Cooking Oil 10 pcs Bamboo Skewers

  • SandWich

    1 pcs of slice bread Mayonnaise, Scrambled Eggs
